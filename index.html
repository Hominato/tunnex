Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to critical operations about the banking sandbox dashboard.

Is this a real banking system?
No. This is a frontend-only MVP (Minimum Viable Product) powered entirely in the browser using localStorage. It does not communicate with any banking server or hold real-world financial records.
How does the mock email system function?
Whenever a transfer, deposit, or login occurs, our SMTP Simulator generates a mock email object and pushes it to your database. You can read these emails inside the Email Center navigation panel.
Will my balance persist if I reload the page?
Yes! All users, settings, and logs are saved in your browser's local storage database, which persists until you clear your browser cache or select "Purge Database" inside Settings.
